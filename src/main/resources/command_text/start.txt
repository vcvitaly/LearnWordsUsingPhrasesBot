Hello! I will try to help you learn new English words!
Please type a word, and I'll send you its definition and an example of its usage.
If you want, you can subscribe to a word pressing the "Subscribe" button under the word.
If you subscribe for a word, you will get a notification with its definition and examples once a day.
You can subscribe for up to 10 words.
There is the "Unsubscribe" button as well.
You can get a list of words you subscribed for by typing /subscribed\_words.
Please type /help to get a list of available commands.
